## **4.1 Primary Objectives**

The primary objectives of the AETHER project are focused on addressing the core challenges identified in earlier chapters and establishing a robust foundation for authorized cybersecurity reconnaissance. The primary objectives are as follows:

1. To design and develop a unified platform for **authorized web attack surface discovery and reconnaissance**.
2. To implement a **focused web crawling mechanism** capable of discovering security-relevant web assets while minimizing unnecessary data collection.
3. To construct **graph-based representations** of web assets and their relationships for enhanced visibility and interpretability.
4. To enable **controlled orchestration of cybersecurity reconnaissance tools** within a sandboxed and resource-limited execution environment.
5. To ensure **legal compliance, safety, and authorization enforcement** throughout the reconnaissance process.
6. To integrate **AI-assisted reasoning** using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) for contextual analysis and explanation of collected security data.

---

## **4.2 Secondary Objectives**

The secondary objectives support the primary goals by enhancing usability, scalability, and analytical depth:

1. To provide an **interactive and user-friendly frontend** for managing projects, scans, and results visualization.
2. To normalize and store reconnaissance outputs in **structured and queryable data formats**.
3. To support **historical tracking and comparison** of reconnaissance results across multiple scans.
4. To enable **natural language querying** of reconnaissance data through a conversational interface.
5. To incorporate **basic prioritization and risk indicators** based on asset relevance and connectivity.
6. To ensure system **modularity and extensibility** for future integration of additional tools and analysis modules.

---

## **4.3 Expected Outcome**

Upon successful implementation, the AETHER project is expected to achieve the following outcomes:

* A functional platform capable of performing **authorized external reconnaissance** on web applications and domains.
* Generation of **structured sitemaps and interactive graphs** that clearly represent web asset relationships.
* Automated and safe execution of reconnaissance tools with **centralized result management**.
* Improved **situational awareness** through AI-assisted summaries and contextual explanations grounded in real scan data.
* Reduction in manual effort required to interpret reconnaissance results.
* A scalable and reusable framework suitable for **defensive security assessment, education, and research purposes**.
